General Description

The project I have decided to work on is a web site dedicated to teach users the sport of volleyball. It will be catered towards people who beginner-level players or who are simply curious about the sport. On the web site, there will be sections that address different facets of the sport. For example, there will be a portion that teach visitors about the basic rules, the basic positions, and the basic skills. In addition, there will be sections that will link topics to respective reference videos and images for visual learners.

Sections/Pages Description

  1. Home
    This will be the landing page of the website where users are presented with an access to all the sections of the site.

  2. About Us
    This will be a page dedicated to introduce who I am and my previous experience as a volleyball player myself.

  3. The Sport
    This section will have all the information about the sport itself.

    • Rules
      This will cover all the rules that a person needs to know before playing.

    • Positions
      This will cover all the different positions that a player may play during a game and their roles.

  4. Basic Skills
    This section will cover all the necessary skills to play.

    • Bumping
      Bumping is the most basic skill ones needs to have.

    • Setting
      This skill is important to set up the ball for hitters to spike the ball.

    • Hitting
      This is the most crucial offensive skill to use in game.

  5. Multimedia
    This portion will contain all the videos and images that may be useful for visitors.

    • Images

    • Videos

    • Youtube Channels
