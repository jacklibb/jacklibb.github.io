General Description

The project I have decided to work on is a web site dedicated to teach users the sport of volleyball. It will be catered towards people who are beginner-level players or who are simply curious about the sport. On the web site, there will be sections that address different facets of the sport. For example, there will be a portion that teaches visitors about the basic rules, the basic positions, and the basic skills. In addition, there will be sections that will link topics to respective reference videos and images for visual learners.

As a volleyball player myself, I wish I had a website that aggregates all the basic information in one location when I first started playing. Thanks to this feature, it will save visitors a lot of time and energy spent researching the web for snippets of information to improve their skills and understanding of the sport. In addition, there will be interactive sections where users will need to provide some input. For example, they might have to analyze a scenario and select the correct answer or they may send requests or questions through the contact form.

Site Information and Other Links

On the top side of all web pages, there will be a standard website banner with the logo and name of the site. On the left-hand side of the pages, there will be an interactive menu that will allow users to access any part of the website by a simple hover and click. On the right-hand side of the pages, there will be a section where all pertinent announcements and volleyball gear sale advertisements are posted for users to see. At the bottom of the pages, users will see useful links to certain portions of the website and to sponsors/interesting links. The middle section will have the bulk of the page's content pertaining to the appropriate subject. There will be embedded images and videos, text, diagrams, etc.

Sections/Pages Description

  1. Home

    This will be the landing page of the website where users are presented with an access to all the sections of the site.

  2. About Us

    This will be a page dedicated to introduce who I am and my previous experience as a volleyball player myself.

  3. The Sport

    This section will have all the information about the sport itself.
    • Rules
       This will cover all the rules that a person needs to know before playing.
    • Positions
      This will cover all the different positions that a player may play during a game and their roles.

  4. Basic Skills

    This section will cover all the necessary skills to play.
    • Bumping
      Bumping is the most basic skill ones needs to have.
    • Setting
      This skill is important to set up the ball for hitters to spike the ball.
    • Hitting
      This is the most crucial offensive skill to use in game.

  5. Multimedia

    This portion will contain all the videos and images that may be useful for visitors.
    • Images
    • Videos
    • Youtube Channels
